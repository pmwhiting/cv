Managing Director of Beech's Confectionery Group

I am the Managing Director at Beech's Confectionery Group, the group specialises in the Manufacture of Branded and Non Branded Confectionery Products. Currently the Group consists of several brands, namely our heritage brand, established in 1920 - Beech's Fine Chocolates, our modern multiple brand - Jenny Wren Chocolates, our luxury "worlds best chocolate" brand - LDN Chocolates and our discount value brand - Belclair. With an annual turnover of over £4M the group provides products to all retail sectors from branded and private label products. Private label producuts range from the top food halls to the top discounters with a product range to suit every market.
